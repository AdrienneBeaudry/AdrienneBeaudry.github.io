Hello!

I'm Adrienne, a sewing and knitting fanatic from Gothenburg, Sweden. I created this blog because I believe that luxury is handsewn. Whether you are en enthousiastic beginner, or an experienced seamstress, I hope that you will leave away feeling deeply inspired and eager to make something with your hands. What if your favourite piece of clothing was made by you?
